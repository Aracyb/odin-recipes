Ratatouille

Ratatouille is a vegetarian French dish made with a variety of vegetables cooked in layers and slowly, resulting in a rich and complex flavor. It's a great option for those seeking a healthy and delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 eggplant
  • 2 zucchini
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs like basil, oregano, or thyme (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preparing the vegetables:
    • Cut all vegetables into thin, uniform slices.
    • The eggplant should be salted and left to rest for 30 minutes to release the bitterness. Then rinse well.
  2. Assembling:
    • In an oiled baking dish, place the vegetable slices in layers, alternating colors and types.
    • Sprinkle each layer with salt, pepper, and chopped fresh herbs.
    • Drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Baking:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for about 40 minutes.
    • Remove the aluminum foil and bake for another 20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and slightly browned.

Tips:

  • Vegetables: You can use other vegetables like pumpkin, sweet potato, or chayote to vary the flavor of your ratatouille.
  • Seasonings: Experiment with different combinations of herbs and spices to customize the flavor of your dish.
  • Side dishes: Ratatouille is delicious served as a side dish with meats, poultry, or fish, or as a main vegetarian dish.

A suggestion: For an even more intense flavor, sauté the onion in olive oil before assembling the layers in the baking dish.

Enjoy!
