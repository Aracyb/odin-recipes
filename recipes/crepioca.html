Delicious Crepioca Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of tapioca flour
  • Salt to taste
  • Filling of your choice (cheese, ham, chicken, etc.)

Preparation:

  1. In a bowl, beat the egg with a fork.
  2. Add the tapioca flour and mix well.
  3. Season with salt to taste.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over low heat.
  5. Pour the batter into the pan, spreading it evenly.
  6. When the crepioca is golden brown on one side, flip it over and add the filling.
  7. Fold the crepioca in half and serve immediately.
