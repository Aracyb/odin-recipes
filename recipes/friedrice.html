Delicious Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, shredded
  • 1/4 cup peas
  • 1/4 cup corn
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the beaten egg and scramble until cooked.
  3. Add the onion, carrot, peas, and corn. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Add the cooked rice and break it up with a fork.
  5. Stir in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, and pepper.
  6. Cook for 1-2 minutes, or until heated through.
  7. Serve hot.
